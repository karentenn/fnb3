Download and link the FNB Banking App

Step 1

Click on the "App Store" icon to access the app store.

Step 2

Search for "FNB" and click on FNB Banking App.

Step 3

Click on INSTALL, and the installation will start.

Step 4

Open the FNB App by clicking on the icon.

Step 5

Click on "Set Up My Banking App".

Step 6

Already have an Online Banking account?
Click on "I have Online Banking" and follow the on-screen instructions.

If you do not, click on "I do not have Online Banking", and follow the on-screen instructions.

NB: The same username and password can be used for the FNB Banking App and Online Banking.
